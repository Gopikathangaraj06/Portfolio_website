AGRIUNITY

Full Stack Web Application (Agriculture + Marketplace)

Description

Farmers frequently face low income due to middlemen, limited market access, and a lack of direct communication with consumers. AGRIUNITY bridges this gap by offering a digital platform that seamlessly connects farmers, consumers, vendors, and experts. By enabling direct farmer-to-consumer sales and providing expert agricultural consultation, the platform ensures transparency, fair pricing, and data-driven insights to improve farming decisions.

Key Features

  • Direct farmer-to-consumer marketplace.
  • Real-time inventory and order management system.
  • Access to expert consultation and agricultural guidance.
  • Sustainability awareness and eco-friendly practices tracking.

Tech Stack

  • Frontend: React.js, HTML, CSS, Tailwind CSS
  • Backend: Node.js, Express.js
  • Database: MySQL

Impact

  • Improves farmer income by eliminating middlemen.
  • Builds trust through a transparent ecosystem.
  • Promotes and rewards sustainable agricultural practices.

PROTEON

Biotechnology + Sustainable Product Development

Description

Tough and coarse meat resulting from storage is a major challenge in the rapidly growing meat industry, where 88% of consumers prefer tender meat. PROTEON solves this by developing a natural, fat-free meat tenderizer using bromelain—a protease enzyme extracted from pineapple crown waste. This innovative waste-to-value approach effectively breaks down collagen, offering an organic and sustainable alternative to physical tenderizers.

Key Features

  • Natural enzyme extraction from pineapple waste.
  • Purified using ammonium sulphate and specific buffers.
  • Enzyme testing verified effectively against casein substrates.
  • Eliminates the need for mechanical force or physical tenderizers.

Tech & Concepts

  • Biotechnology & Enzyme Kinetics
  • Sustainable Waste-to-Value Processing
  • Eco-friendly & Organic Formulations

Impact & Scope

  • Significantly reduces agricultural waste.
  • Provides a cost-effective, safer, fat-free alternative.
  • Highly scalable for future automated industrial systems.
